Drink Outside the Box

Get ready for an adventurous evening at "Drink Outside the Box" in the heart of our dormitory! This event promises to be a thrilling experience that encourages you to step out of your comfort zone and try a variety of unconventional and creative drinks while mingling with fellow residents and friends.

Date: Saturday, November 25, 2023
Time: 20:00
Location: Common room

Explore a unique menu of out-of-the-ordinary beverages, from exotic cocktails to unusual mocktails. Whether you're an adventurous drink enthusiast or just looking for an intriguing way to spend an evening, there's something to pique your curiosity.

As the night unfolds, you can participate in interactive mixology sessions and learn to craft your own experimental drinks. Our skilled mixologists will guide you through the process, ensuring you leave with newfound skills and unforgettable memories.

Don't miss the opportunity to expand your horizons and bond with your fellow residents while sipping on extraordinary concoctions. "Drink Outside the Box" promises to be an evening of creativity and camaraderie, so come prepared for an unforgettable journey into the world of innovative beverages. We can't wait to see you there!
