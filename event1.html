Gin&Game

11th of November

Join us for a thrilling and fun-filled evening at "Gin & Game Night" in the heart of our dormitory! This unique event promises to be an unforgettable experience that brings together residents and friends for an evening of laughter, competition, and camaraderie.

Get ready to roll the dice, strategize, and enjoy a variety of board games, card games, and party games. Whether you're a seasoned board game enthusiast or just looking for a relaxed way to unwind with friends, there's something for everyone. From classic games like Monopoly and Scrabble to modern favorites like Codenames and Cards Against Humanity, we've got a game for every taste.

As the night unfolds, you can savor the delightful taste of gin and other refreshing beverages at our cozy lounge area. Enjoy your favorite gin and tonic or experiment with creative gin-based cocktails crafted by our talented mixologists.

Date: Saturday, November 11, 2023
Time: 20:00
Location: Common room

 Don't miss the chance to bond with your fellow residents and make new friends while sipping on gin and enjoying some friendly competition. Gin & Game Night promises to be an evening of relaxation and entertainment, so come prepared for a fantastic time in the comfort of your dormitory. We can't wait to see you there!
