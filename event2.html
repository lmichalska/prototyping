Martinis at Midnight

18th of November

Join us for a glamorous and enchanting evening at "Martinis at Midnight" in the heart of our dormitory! This sophisticated event promises to transport you to a world of elegance, offering a night of indulgence and connection with fellow residents and friends.

Get ready to sip on exquisite martinis and enjoy the ambiance of a refined cocktail lounge. Whether you're a seasoned martini connoisseur or just looking for a chic way to unwind with friends, you'll find a martini to suit your taste, from classic options to creative concoctions.

Date: Saturday, November 18, 2023
Time: 20:00
Location: Common room

As the night unfolds, revel in the sophistication and the mesmerizing melodies of a live jazz band that will set the perfect mood for this late-night affair. Dress to impress and immerse yourself in the magic of "Martinis at Midnight."

Don't miss the chance to create lasting memories with your fellow residents and make new friends while enjoying the elegance of martinis and live music. "Martinis at Midnight" promises to be an evening of class and style, so come prepared to be part of this unforgettable experience. We look forward to seeing you there!
