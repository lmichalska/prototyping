Black & White Night

Embrace the elegance and sophistication of "Black & White Night" at our dormitory! This event promises to transport you to a world of timeless style and create lasting memories with fellow residents and friends.

Date: Friday, December 2, 2023
Time: 20:00
Location: Common room

Dress in your finest black and white attire and step into a monochromatic wonderland. Experience a night of timeless class and style as you mingle and dance to the tunes of classic and modern music in a beautifully decorated setting.

Enjoy a selection of delectable black and white-themed cocktails and appetizers. Whether you're a connoisseur of fine beverages or just looking to indulge in an evening of glamour, you'll find an array of elegant drink options to satisfy your taste.

Don't miss the opportunity to create memories with your fellow residents, make new friends, and immerse yourself in the elegance of "Black & White Night." This event promises to be an evening of sophistication and camaraderie, so come prepared for a night of timeless enchantment. We can't wait to see you there!
